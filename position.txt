About Cignus

Cignus Consulting is an aviation technology firm delivering data-driven solutions that improve safety, efficiency, and operational performance across airports and aviation ecosystems. We work at the intersection of aviation operations, cloud platforms, analytics, and advanced decision-support systems - helping our clients move from fragmented data to actionable intelligence. Our clients include the FAA, NASA, Department of Defense, and major airports across the United States.

 

The Opportunity

We are seeking motivated and talented interns to join our engineering team for Summer 2026. You will work directly alongside senior engineers and aviation domain experts to build core components of our AI platform. This is a hands-on role where your contributions will make it into production systems used by real-world aviation stakeholders.

This is an ideal opportunity for students who want to apply their technical skills to meaningful, real-world problems in a fast-paced, collaborative environment. Aviation knowledge is not required but is considered a plus.

 

What You’ll Work On

Depending on your skills and interests, your work may span several of the following areas:

Data API Development: Design, build, and document RESTful APIs that connect diverse aviation data sources (flight data, weather, NOTAMs, airport operations, ADS-B) into a unified platform layer
RAG System Development: Build and refine Retrieval-Augmented Generation pipelines that power our domain-specific AI assistant, including document ingestion, chunking strategies, vector storage, and retrieval optimization
AI Skills & Agents: Develop modular AI “skills”—structured prompt-and-tool packages that enable our platform to perform specialized aviation analysis tasks autonomously
MCP Server Development: Build Model Context Protocol servers that allow AI agents to interact with external tools, data sources, and enterprise systems in standardized ways
Cloud Infrastructure: Work with Azure cloud services to deploy, monitor, and scale platform components including APIs, databases, and AI inference pipelines
Frontend & Visualization: Contribute to dashboards and user interfaces that surface AI-generated insights for aviation professionals
Data Engineering: Build data pipelines for ingestion, transformation, and enrichment of aviation datasets using Python and cloud-native tools
